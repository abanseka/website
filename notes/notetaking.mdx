# How to take notes

this guide explains the QEC method of taking smart notes taken down
by restructuring the thoughts of the author into your own thoughts.

## QEC

- Q -> Question
- E -> Evidence
- C -> Conclusion

### ATOMICITY

- byte sized ideas in a mind map like layout
- thinking in three 3D avoiding the vertical contraints of
  regular top -> bottom layout note systems
